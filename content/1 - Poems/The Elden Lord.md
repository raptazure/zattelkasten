O miserable cacophony! 

Blood and War endless!

"Ye shalt become the Elden Lord."

Thou killed a God with a broken sword.

But for what reward?

No redemption dwells in massacre

Nor thy restless flame of valour.

Meaningless void stained thy conquer,

Thou art an Elden Lord of Death.
