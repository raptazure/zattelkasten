Write, write incessantly,   
Until every word evaporates.   
  
Be it a fading coal,   
Glowing in the dark valley,   
  
Or a butterfly,   
Dissolving in Winter's memory,   
  
Or a casted spell,   
Flung into the oblivion of Abyss.   
  
Live, live incessantly.   
The answer there will be.
