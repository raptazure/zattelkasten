- *– To Imagination*

O ever-present Deity,

How I love thee almighty!

When thy eminence flows into my heart,

Leading me to the Aonian Mount,

Every fetter I break apart.

<br>

Upsoaring with immense rapture,

Roaming freely upon dreams’ azure,

I am my soul’s master,

Regardless of weather.
