- 2024.6.30

O, Gentle Soul,  
Gently breathing sorrow,  
  
Sighing like a Deluge foretold  
By the Lightening fierce and bold  
Bursting through the purple Nights of Old  
  
Before the Crescent Moon wore her garments cold.
