Death marching on the barren land,

Screams of sorrow swam in the sky.

Chaos commanding the warriors mad,

Void of light I cannot see why.

War, War, eternal War, 

seemingly irreconcilable.

If only you know the meaning of Might

And flowers' dance in Spring so Bright.
