Experience is the dance between the perceiving intellect and the thing being perceived.

Experience is the alchemic process, the turning of the raw material, the transformation in flames.

Experience is the meaning attached to the world.

Experience is the perfect blending.

Be patient until beauty grows out of our gaze.
